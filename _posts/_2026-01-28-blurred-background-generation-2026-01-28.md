# Blurred Background
In Lily Fantasia, the players have the option to set "background blur intensity" and "color balance intensity."

"Background Blur Intensity" determines how blurry the background is. The higher it goes, the more blurry the background becomes.

On the other hand, "Color Balance Intensity" determines how "color balanced" the background looks. In short, this setting makes the darker backgrounds look brighter when it's set to high
